Erin Neitzel

Erin Neitzel is a filmmaker, editor, projectionist & curator once in Colorado and now in NYC. 

Her work focuses on creating spaces for DIY no-budget experimental filmmakers + video artists artists, and reconciling the temporal gaps in various mediums of film technology.
